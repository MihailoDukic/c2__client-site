The C2 Experience

×

Welcome to the immersive, one-of-a-kind event that will help transform the way you do business.

Each year, C2 Montréal brings more than 7,500 talented creatives, bold entrepreneurs, industry leaders and other forward-thinkers together at the intersection of commerce and creativity. Over the course of three days, they will connect, learn, collaborate and build the new set of tools they need to take on the challenges of our times, unlock new business opportunities and participate in shaping the future.


In the wake of COVID-19, the world changed and so has business. Challenging times demand creative solutions: enter C2 Online – Montréal 2020, a new way to convene and connect. C2 Online is a completely digital event separate from the annual C2 Montréal conference, albeit with the world-class speakers, solution-driven workshops, exclusive experiences and signature creative C2 content you’ve come to expect. Participants benefit from enhanced digital content, deeper learning, heightened networking and greater community engagement.



Inspiring talks

Be inspired by the world's top thinkers


C2 speakers are an engaging mix of business innovators, social game-changers and world-class experts — the latest and greatest in many diverse fields as well as the next generation of leading-edge entrepreneurs. Their talks are an invitation to find inspiration in their stories, and an open doorway into deeper exploration of the topics raised.



Signature experiences

Learn.Connect.Experience.


Signature experiences encourage meaningful interactions, collaborations and foster shared learning. Each of the immersive signature experiences offered to participants will focus on one of these three pillars: Learn, Connect and Experience.



Masterclasses

Turning words into action


Masterclasses are interactive meetups designed to bring experts and participants closer together around topical and applicable subject matter. This unique format relies on artistic direction, performances, targeted content and collaborative work that explores a chosen topic more deeply.



Hands-on workshops

Getting your hands dirty


Offered in collaboration with experts, a broad range of workshops invite you to roll up your sleeves and work with other participants in prototyping sessions that address real-world business challenges, experiment with new creative processes and put ideas into practice.



Curated one-on-ones

Meeting the right people


Each year, C2 Montréal gathers thousands of innovative thinkers from around the world. When you’re in such good company, you could potentially learn more from the person sitting right next to you than from the person on stage. Engineering business-changing connections and having participants meet that person they didn’t know they needed to meet – perhaps a future business soulmate or even a best friend – is at the heart of the C2 experience, so every element of the event is designed to maximize meaningful connection opportunities that appeal to all participants. And new in 2020, the C2Connect platform, accessible through your smart badge, uses technology that allows you to have meetups with people who share your interests, making curated networking easy.



A whole village to discover

Go down the rabbit hole


Every year, the setting for C2 Montréal is reinvented to create the perfect playground for you to come and kick your ideas around in. The vibrant, one-of-a-kind C2 village, as it is called, is designed to encourage discovery and spark conversations. It’s a place where curiosity is the norm, where risk is encouraged, where being bold is celebrated and where anything can happen.



Artistic performances

Expecting the unexpected


Live performances (some announced, some not) punctuate the event. A multitude of artists — musical, visual, circus, dance and more — not only stoke the fires of stimulation, but also serve as entertaining and thought-provoking metaphors for some of the other big C’s at C2: connection, collision and collaboration.



Montreal-style festivities

Celebrate your successes


Celebration is deep in the C2 DNA, and provides opportunities for all to connect on another level. Entertainment-filled evenings showcasing leading international and local artists and performers have included the world-renowned talents of Cirque du Soleil, Moment Factory, Cirque Éloize, Snoop Dogg, LCD Soundsystem’s James Murphy, Win Butler of Arcade Fire, and Moby, to name a few.



Art and technology exhibits

Explore the intersection of art and business


Carefully curated works by cutting edge innovators from around the world offer powerful commentaries while demonstrating the creative links between industry, technology and culture.



Born and raised in Montréal

Blending influences and ideas


C2 was created in Montreal, and that is no coincidence. Montreal mixes cultures and languages, bridges America and Europe, flirts with the old and the new. Much like this natural hub for creativity and innovation, C2 is a blend of influences, a cross between disciplines, a hybrid creature that pulls divergent ideas together and helps turn them into exciting opportunities.
