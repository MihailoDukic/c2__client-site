Who attends and why


C2 Montréal: Where great minds meet


C2 Montréal is for individuals and organizations that have a strong desire to connect, learn, collaborate and discover the inspiration to take on challenges, unlock new business opportunities and participate in shaping the future. The three-day event brings industry leaders, innovators, entrepreneurs, creatives and other forward-thinkers together at the intersection of commerce and creativity with the goal of bettering both business and society.

At C2 Montréal you can expect to:


  • - Build the tools you need to better face change in your industry
  • - Connect and collaborate with potential business partners and mentors
  • - Learn about future trends, advances and disruptions
  • Explore new ideas and insights from a broad range of industries and disciplines
  • - Learn hands-on methods for finding creative solutions to business problems
  • - Help your business discover – or become – the next big thing
  • - Leave with actionable ideas you can put into practice with your team

Why come as a group?


Attending C2 Montréal as a group has clear benefits, not the least of which is being able to profit fully from an experience tailored to your business. It’s about getting your team and organization inspired, exploring new creative processes and maximizing the wealth of intellect and ability you have at hand. All of which is not an easy thing to do by yourself. So put more boots on the ground – bring your own army.
